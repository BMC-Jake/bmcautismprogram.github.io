About the App


This app provides a collection of social stories about going to the doctor to help people with Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) prepare for their doctor’s appointments.
  • Social stories detail each step of a situation in order help ease anxiety and set expectations about what will happen.
The social stories in this app are about:
  • Getting to the doctor’s office on the campus of Boston Medical Center (BMC)

  • How common medical procedures— getting a shot; drawing blood; and measuring height, weight, and blood pressure— are conducted
